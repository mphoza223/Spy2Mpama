Spy2 Mpama

Spy2 Mpama (1.0.0) is a fun, easy to play soccer-based game. With a notable diversion from it's namesake, it was meant to capture the excitement of the original game. The real spy2 mpama involves a group of players passing a soccer ball around trying to steer it to go through an opponent's legs. If one succeeds in doing this, everybody gets the right to slap the loser. Fortunately this version has no violence . Enjoy.
